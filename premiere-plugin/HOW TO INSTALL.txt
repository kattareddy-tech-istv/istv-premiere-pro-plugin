================================================================
  INSIDE SUCCESS TV — AI CUT SHEET PLUGIN
  Installation Guide for Editors
================================================================

WHAT IS THIS?
-------------
This plugin adds an AI-powered editing assistant directly inside
Adobe Premiere Pro. It can:

  • Generate a cut sheet from your interview audio in minutes
  • Automatically apply cuts to your multicam timeline
  • Suggest edit points based on camera angles and pacing
  • Detect and mark silences in any clip
  • Apply all cuts from a cut sheet with one click

You do NOT need to know how to code. Just follow the steps below.


----------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 1 — INSTALL THE PLUGIN (Windows)
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Right-click the file called  install.bat
2. Choose "Run as administrator"
   (If Windows asks "Do you want to allow this app to make
   changes?" — click YES)

3. A black window will open and run automatically.
   Wait until it says "Installation Complete!"

4. Press any key to close the window.

5. FULLY CLOSE and REOPEN Adobe Premiere Pro.
   (Don't just minimize it — actually quit and restart.)

6. In Premiere Pro, go to:
      Window  →  Extensions  →  "Inside Success TV — Cut Sheet"

   The panel will appear. You're almost done!


----------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 1 — INSTALL THE PLUGIN (Mac)
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Open Terminal
   (Press Cmd + Space, type "Terminal", press Enter)

2. Type this command and press Enter:
      bash /path/to/install.sh

   Or just drag the install.sh file into the Terminal window
   after typing "bash " (with a space), then press Enter.

3. Restart Adobe Premiere Pro.

4. Window → Extensions → "Inside Success TV — Cut Sheet"


----------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 2 — ENTER YOUR BACKEND URL
----------------------------------------------------------------

When the panel opens for the first time, you'll see a
"Backend" section at the top.

1. Type in the URL your team lead gave you.
   It looks something like:
      https://istv-pipeline.onrender.com
   or
      http://192.168.1.10:8000  (if it's on your local network)

2. Click  SAVE

3. Click  TEST CONNECTION
   You should see "Backend online" in green.
   If you see an error, double-check the URL with your team lead.


----------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 3 — YOU'RE READY!
----------------------------------------------------------------

The plugin is now fully set up. Here's a quick overview
of what each section does:

  1 — Audio File
      Browse for your raw interview audio file and upload it.
      The system will transcribe it automatically.

  2 — Transcript
      Shows the transcription status. Takes 2-10 minutes
      depending on the length of your audio.

  3 — Generate Cut Sheet
      Pick your documentary format (12-15 min, 15 min, etc.)
      and click "Generate Cut Sheet". Claude AI will analyze
      the transcript and create a full editorial cut sheet.

  4 — Import to Premiere Pro
      Imports an XML timeline based on the cut sheet directly
      into your open Premiere Pro project.

  5 — Apply Cut Sheet to Timeline
      Paste in any cut sheet text and the plugin reads all
      the [IP @ timestamps] and applies razor cuts to your
      synced multicam timeline automatically.

  Multicam AI Edit
      Scans your open sequence, sends it to Claude, and gets
      suggested cut points. You approve or reject each one,
      then apply with one click.

  Edit Selected Clip
      Click on a clip in your timeline, then click
      "Get Selected Clip" here. The plugin can detect silences
      and help you clean up individual clips.


----------------------------------------------------------------
  HOW UPDATES WORK (Nothing you need to do!)
----------------------------------------------------------------

When your team lead pushes an update, you'll see a blue banner
at the top of the plugin that says:

  "Plugin update available — click to install"

Just click  UPDATE NOW  and wait about 10 seconds.
Then press F5 (or close and reopen the panel).

That's it. No downloading a new ZIP. No re-running install.bat.
Updates happen automatically through the internet.


----------------------------------------------------------------
  TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------------------------------------------------------

PROBLEM: I don't see the plugin in Window → Extensions
FIX: Re-run install.bat as Administrator, then fully restart
     Premiere Pro. Make sure you close it completely.

PROBLEM: "Backend unreachable" error
FIX: Check the URL in the Backend field. Make sure you have
     internet access. Contact your team lead for the correct URL.

PROBLEM: "No active sequence" when I try to scan
FIX: Click on a sequence tab in Premiere Pro to make it the
     active sequence, then try again.

PROBLEM: The cuts aren't applying to my timeline
FIX: Use "Markers Only" first to preview where the cuts would go.
     Some Premiere versions have restrictions on razor cuts via
     scripts. If markers work but cuts don't, apply the cuts
     manually using the markers as guides.

PROBLEM: The panel is blank / white
FIX: In Chrome, go to http://localhost:7777 to open the debug
     console. Or try closing and reopening the panel.

PROBLEM: CSInterface.js is missing
FIX: The installer tries to download it automatically. If it
     failed, download it manually from:
     https://github.com/Adobe-CEP/CEP-Resources
     (click CEP_12.x → CSInterface.js → Download raw file)
     Save it inside your plugin folder.

PROBLEM: "No transcript available" when generating cut sheet
FIX: You need to upload and transcribe your audio file first
     (Steps 1 and 2 in the panel). The transcript must be
     ready before you can generate a cut sheet.
